About Me

I received my B.S. and M.S. from Zhengzhou University, China, in 2008 and 2011, respectively, and my Ph.D. from Temple University in 2016, all in Computer Science. I joined Zhengzhou University in 2017, where I am an Associate Professor; from 2016 to 2017, I worked at Amazon. My research interests include Computer Vision, Robot Perception, and Deep Learning.
